The Fun Stuff

When I'm not doing math, you can typically find me exclaiming at my computer screen over a Starcraft/chess tournament. I also spend some time coaching a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu class once a week. Since I moved to Germany, I have been biking around, a lot. I've also been hiking a lot. I've been trying to learn how to cook and have pretty much mastered a beef stew. I haven't been disciplined about learning German, but my wife and I watched Breaking Bad in German and it still slapped. Plus now I know they say "Waffe" for "gun" and "Meth" for "meth," so I'm basically good to go.
