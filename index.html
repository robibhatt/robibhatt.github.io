I'm a third year PhD student at UC San Diego, co-advised by Kamalika Chaudhuri and Sanjoy Dasgupta. I'm broadly interested in various topics in theoretical machine learning. Lately, I've done some work in adversarial robustness as well as online k-means clustering. I also like math, in which I receieved a B.S. from MIT in 2016.
